About E-Waste

Understanding the E-Waste Crisis

Electronic waste is the fastest growing waste stream globally — and India is at the center of this storm.

⚠️

What is E-Waste?

Electronic waste (e-waste) includes discarded electrical or electronic devices — phones, computers, TVs, refrigerators, batteries — that have reached end of life.

LeadMercuryCadmiumArsenicChromium
☠️

Why It's Dangerous

Improper disposal releases toxic chemicals into soil and groundwater, causing cancer, neurological damage, and respiratory diseases — especially in informal recycling zones.

78% disposed informally in India

A sustainable ecosystem where nothing is wasted — every device has a second life.

Reduce

Buy less, buy better. Opt for durable, repairable devices. Avoid unnecessary upgrades and plan for longevity.

Reuse
